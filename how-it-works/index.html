How it works

How your home
sells for
1% + marketing.

A clear, modern sales process from appraisal to settlement — built to present your home properly, attract serious buyers and negotiate the best possible result.

The process — step by step
01
Accurate pricing from day one
We assess your home using comparable local sales, current market movement and the details that make your property different. The goal is simple: launch with a price strategy grounded in reality, not a number designed to win a listing. You get a realistic price range and a clear recommended approach — no inflated appraisal, no correction three weeks later.
Comparable Sales AnalysisSuburb Trend DataData-Informed Pricing
02
Presentation that gets buyers to stop scrolling
We prepare your campaign assets properly — photography, floor plans and walkthroughs — so your property looks considered, polished and inspection-worthy from the first click. Professional shoot, expert enhancement, virtual staging where appropriate. No upsells, no add-ons. Every listing gets the same treatment.
Professional PhotographyVirtual StagingEnhanced Presentation
03
Clear floor plans buyers actually use
Every listing includes a professionally generated floor plan — accurate, clean, and ready for every portal and print material. Buyers want to understand the layout before they visit. A good floor plan filters out time-wasters and brings in qualified buyers who already know the property suits them.
Floor Plan IncludedPortal-Ready Format
04
Optional Matterport 3D walkthrough
If you want to add a fully interactive Matterport 3D tour, we can arrange it as an affordable add-on. Buyers navigate every room, measure spaces, and understand the flow of the property — all before setting foot inside. For interstate buyers, it's often what turns interest into an offer. It's not included as standard, but it's not expensive and it pays for itself.
Matterport 3D TourRoom MeasurementInterstate-Ready
05
A campaign built to reach the right buyers
Your property goes live across every major portal — realestate.com.au, domain.com.au, and more — the same day photography is ready. You choose your portal exposure level: Standard (~$600–$900), Feature (~$1,200–$1,800), or Premiere (~$2,000–$2,500+). This is the only pass-through cost outside the 1%. Simultaneously, a targeted digital campaign launches to reach qualified buyers who match your property profile.
realestate.com.audomain.com.auTargeted Digital Ads
06
Responsive enquiry and inspection management
Every buyer enquiry gets a fast, accurate response. We make sure buyers get the information they need quickly, inspection opportunities are handled properly, and interest doesn't go cold through poor follow-up. Inspections are managed efficiently, and as a seller you see a real-time picture of buyer activity.
Fast Enquiry ResponseInspection ManagementBuyer Follow-Up
07
Offer management and negotiation
When offers come in, we help you compare them clearly, understand the conditions behind them, and negotiate from a position of confidence. No phone tag, no runaround. You get clear advice on every offer, counter-offer, and condition — with your interests and your best outcome as the only objective.
Offer AssessmentCondition NegotiationSeller-First Advice
08
Contract to settlement support
Once your property is under contract, we stay close to the process and keep things moving toward settlement. Building and pest, finance conditions, settlement adjustments, and handover — all coordinated, all included. One flat 1% commission covers everything from first conversation to final handover. No surprises, no extras.
Settlement CoordinationFinance ConditionsHandover Support
